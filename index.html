Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your Gift Card.

Where do I find my card number?

Your 16-digit card number is printed on the front of your Gift Card.

Where is the PIN located?

The PIN/CVV is on the back of your card. You may need to scratch off the silver coating to reveal it.

Is it safe to check my balance here?

Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption for all data transmissions. Your card details are never stored on our servers after the balance check is complete.

Why does my card show a zero balance?

A zero balance can occur if the card has not been activated, the funds have been fully spent, or the card has expired.

What card types are supported?

We support Visa and Mastercard gift cards and prepaid cards.

Is there a fee to check my balance?

No. Checking your gift card balance is completely free of charge.
